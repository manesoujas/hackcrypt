About Us

Learn more about our mission and vision

Thakur Ramnarayan College of Arts and Commerce provides a vibrant and inclusive academic environment that supports student growth, faculty research, and community engagement. TRCAC emphasizes collaboration, personal development, and diverse perspectives to prepare students for a rapidly evolving world. Our Arts, Science, and Commerce programs focus on building critical thinking, strong communication, and a well-rounded foundation for professional success and responsible citizenship.

About TRCAC

DevClub TRCAC, under the B.Sc. (I.T.) and B.Sc. (C.S.) Department of TRCAC, is a dynamic club that nurtures creativity, technical skills, and teamwork. It hosts departmental events, competitions, and major hackathons while preparing students for external tech challenges. Its flagship ZETTAABYTE Tech Fest offers coding contests, robotics events, workshops, and tech talks—providing students with hands-on learning, networking opportunities, and a strong foundation to excel in the fast-evolving tech world.
